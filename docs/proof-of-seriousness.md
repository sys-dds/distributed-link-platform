# Proof of Seriousness

DLP is serious because it models the concerns that appear after a simple backend already works.

## Serious signals

- reserved routes and self-redirect protection
- RFC 7807-style errors
- health/metrics/logging
- lifecycle-aware links
- cache miss/hit proof
- async Kafka analytics
- outbox leases, parking, poison handling, backpressure
- projection rebuild/replay
- owner/workspace governance
- hashed keys, service accounts, permissions
- usage ledger, entitlements, exact quota races
- webhooks with signing, verification, health, replay
- exports/imports/retention/recovery drills
- query fallback and replica lag policy
- redirect failover and fail-closed proof
- abuse intelligence and global governance
